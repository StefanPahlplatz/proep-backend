Car pick up instructions

Since you make this reservation your car will be only available to you and this reservation can not be cancelled. During the day you rent the car, your car will be located exactly on the stated address mentioned in this email. Here is the procedures:

  • Before the day you rent the car, your car host will make sure the car is in healthy condition and you have a pleasant trip
  • Make sure to pick up the car on time on both of you had agreed (also for return). Keep it casual and have a little chat with the owner
  • Together with the owner, you will check the car and its surrounds to make sure that any damaged you found during checking that not caused by you.
  • You might also want to ask other things such as car manuals, what fuel does the car consumes and etc before you go
  • Car host will hand in you the key after everything is clear and you are welcome to start your trip.
  • When something is wrong at this point, or when something is unclear or you are not able to work things out, you can contact our Community Support. They are very welcome

Car return instructions

After you have done with your trip then you need to deliver back the car again to the place where exactly you pick up the car. There are some procedures need to be followed

  • Make sure to return the car on time on both you had agreed. Inform an owner if you have not returned in time, it's always good to keep communication
  • Lateness will be counted as an extra day fare, so please be aware of your time
  • After you have reached your drop out point, owner will ask you the key and do some inspection within the car
  • In case there is any damage on the car that you made, owner will count on how much damage it will costs to be a penalty you need to be paid
  • Once everything is clear and your rental will be ended automatically
  • That was it! Your first rental has finished! You can now leave a review of the car and owner if you wish
  • When something is unclear or if you have any questions, you are always welcome to contact our custome support service
